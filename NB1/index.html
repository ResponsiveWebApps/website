Reserve Your Interplanetary Address

A blank-slate, global directory intended to upgrade and replace the existing DNS using the Ethereum blockchain. Nebulis is compatible with a wide variety of content-addressed protocols like IPFS and MaidSafe.

Start
lock_outline

Secure

The Nebulis directory has minimal reliance on caching, so users can query the canonical blockchain record without imposing excess costs or traffic on the system.

blur_on

Decentralized

Nebulis has a federated structure, where clusters are owned by those who create them. Domain records are always under the complete control of the owner.

trending_up

Scalable

With the introduction of sharding and proof of stake, the Ethereum blockchain is scalable to the requirements of a global directory will millions of records.

A massive Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack against Dyn, a major domain name system (DNS) provider, broke large portions of the Internet on Friday, causing a significant outage to a ton of websites and services, including Twitter, GitHub, PayPal, Amazon, Reddit, Netflix, and Spotify. But how the attack happened? What's the cause behind the attack? Exact details of the attack remain vague, but Dyn reported a huge army of hijacked internet-connected devices could be responsible for the massive attack.
